✅ Study Tips for BDA

  • Unit 2 & 5 are CRITICAL: Together worth 32 marks (45% of total). Focus here first.
  • Diagrams Matter: Unit 2 & 5 have heavy diagram questions. Study and reproduce HDFS, Hadoop Ecosystem, MapReduce flow, YARN, and Spark architecture.
  • Differentiation Questions: CAP Theorem, Structured vs Unstructured data, Hive vs Pig - appear frequently. Always use table format.
  • Architecture Questions: Questions 302, 303, 304, 307, 605, 608 account for significant marks. Understand component relationships deeply.
  • Real-World Applications: Every concept connects to real problems (fraud detection, streaming analytics, data warehousing). Relate answers to practical use.
